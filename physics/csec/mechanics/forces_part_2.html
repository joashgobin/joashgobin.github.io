3.2 Weight Solutions

The following are the solutions to the mission given in the lesson on Forces.

  1. 3 kg bag of rice $$\begin{equation}\begin{aligned} W&=mg\ &=3\ kg\times 9.81\ ms^{-2}\ &=29.43\ kgms^{-2}\ OR\ 29.43\ N \end{aligned}\end{equation}$$

  2. 0.4 kg ball $$\begin{equation}\begin{aligned} W&=mg\ &=0.4\ kg\times 9.81\ ms^{-2}\ &=3.924\ kgms^{-2}\ OR\ 3.924\ N \end{aligned}\end{equation}$$

  3. 50 g coin $$\begin{equation}\begin{aligned} 50\ g&\rightarrow 0.05\ kg\ W&=mg\ &=0.05\ kg\times 9.81\ ms^{-2}\ &=0.4905\ N\rightarrow 0.4905\times 10^0\ N \rightarrow4.905\times 10^{-1}\ N \end{aligned}\end{equation}$$

Notice how we have to ensure that mass is in standard units before we can use the formula for weight.
